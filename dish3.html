    • Pasta Carbonara


    How to make the best spaghetti carbonara from scratch with only five ingredients. This homemade carbonara is made with nutty parmigiano reggiano cheese, pancetta, spaghetti, creamy egg yolks, and black pepper.

    Prep time

    10 min.

    Cook Time

    15 min.

    Portions

    4

    Instruction

    Step 1

    Bring 6 quarts of water to a boil in a large pot over high heat. Add a generous amount of salt to the pot.

    Step 2

    Stir spaghetti into the boiling water and cook it according to the package instructions.

    Step 3

    While the pasta is cooking, cook pancetta, guancile, or bacon in a separate pan until it is cooked through. Drain it on a paper towel-lined plate.

    Step 4

    In a bowl, stir together egg yolks, egg, and parmigiano reggiano cheese until combined. Stir in black pepper.

    Step 5

    Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and toss it with the cooked pancetta (or guancile).

    Step 6

    Ladle in the egg and cheese mixture, a little at a time, and toss the pasta until the mixture coats it evenly.

    Step 7

    If needed, stir in 1/2 cup of the reserved pasta water to thin out the sauce and help it coat the pasta better.

    Step 8

    Serve with freshly cracked black pepper and grated cheese.


    Ingredient

    4 ounces Pancetta (or Guanciale or Bacon)

    1 cup Parmigiano Reggiano

    3 large Egg Yolks

    1 large Egg

    12 ounces Spaghetti

    ¼ teaspoon Black Pepper

    ½ cup Pasta Water


    Utensil

    non stick skillets

    mixing bowl

    spatula

    fork
