    • Fettuccine Alfredo


    There are certain recipes that are essential to have in your recipe box and one of them has to be a phenomenal Fettuccine alfredo recipe. Years ago, when I was teaching kids' cooking classes, this was one of the first things I taught them. Almost every single kid around goes crazy for alfredo sauce! It is so easy to make a homemade alfredo sauce that you will never buy the jarred variety again!

    Prep time

    15 min.

    Cook Time

    15 min.

    Portions

    4

    Instruction

    Step 1

    Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil over high heat. Season the water with salt.

    Step 2

    Add fettuccine to the boiling water and cook it according to the instructions on the package.

    Step 3

    In a large skillet or pan, melt butter over medium heat.

    Step 4

    Add minced garlic to the skillet and cook for 1 to 2 minutes, stirring constantly.

    Step 5

    Stir in heavy cream and let it cook for 5 to 8 minutes until it reduces.

    Step 6

    Add half of the parmesan cheese to the skillet and whisk well until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.

    Step 7

    Drain the cooked fettuccine, reserving some pasta water. Toss the pasta with the alfredo sauce in the skillet.


    Ingredient

    1 lb Fettuccine Pasta

    6 Tablespoons Butter

    1 Garlic Clove (minced)

    1 ½ cups Heavy Cream

    ¼ teaspoon Salt

    1 ¼ cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese

    ¼ teaspoon Pepper

    2 Tablespoons Italian Parsley


    Utensil

    knife

    measuring cup

    spatula

    spoon
